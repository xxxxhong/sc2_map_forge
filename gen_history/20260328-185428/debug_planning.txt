### Battle Scenario: "Echoes of Deception"

#### Strategy: 声东击西 (Feint to the East, Attack to the West)

This battle scenario is designed to implement the Chinese military stratagem 声东击西, which involves creating a diversion to mislead the enemy and striking at a target they did not anticipate. In this Terran vs. Zerg match-up, you will utilize a feint to draw Zerg forces to one flank while your main force strikes at a critical point.

---

#### Force Composition

**Terran (Player)**
- 4 Marines: Main offensive unit, high mobility for versatile engagement.
- 2 Marauders: Provide heavy fire support against armored Zerg units.
- 2 Siege Tanks: Offer long-range bombardment capabilities.
- 1 Medivac: Ensure unit longevity through on-field healing.
- 1 Reaper: Acts as a scout and secondary feint unit due to its high mobility.

**Zerg (Enemy)**
- 5 Zerglings: Quick response and harassment units.
- 2 Roaches: Provide tankiness and pressure against Terran infantry.
- 2 Hydralisks: Offer ranged attack to challenge Terran's aerial capabilities.
- 1 Mutalisk: Adds air threat to Terran ground units.

---

#### Positioning and Phases

**Phase 1: Initial Deployment**
- **Terran**: Position Marines and Marauders near the eastern edge of the map to simulate a forthcoming assault. Deploy the Reaper for rapid scouting and feint execution near the eastern frontlines.
- **Zerg**: Scatter Zerglings on key choke points, with Roaches in support positions and Hydralisks covering flanks. Hold Mutalisk in reserve near the main base for flexibility.

**Phase 2: Execute Feint (Voice)**
- Use the Reaper to launch initial harassment and reconnaissance on the eastern front, creating noise (“声”) and drawing Zerg units toward defending this perceived main attack.
- Initiate bombardment with Siege Tanks concealed behind cover near the central region, suggesting long-range support for the eastern assault.

**Phase 3: Main Assault (West Strike)**
- Deploy the bulk of Terran forces, including Medivac-supported Marines and Marauders, to strike at an exposed western Zerg structure or high-value resource point, executing the actual attack (“击西”).
- Utilize terrain cover as listed in XML to approach undetected, ensuring maximum surprise.

**Terrain Utilization Notes:**
- Leverage cliffs and high ground as described in the XML to provide Siege Tanks an advantageous firing position.
- Utilize valleys and narrow passages to funnel and control Zergling mobility.
- Use Medivac to allow flexible unit repositioning across uneven terrain, bypassing obstacles described in the terrain file.

---

#### Victory/Defeat Conditions

**Victory Conditions:**
- Destroy the Zerg’s vital structure or resource cache located on the western flank.
- Complete mission objectives within 10 minutes after the main strike to demonstrate swift execution of the stratagem.

**Defeat Conditions:**
- Loss of all Siege Tanks before the main assault.
- Complete annihilation of the main western attack force.

---

This scenario allows players to master the art of deception and surprise, leveraging terrain and strategic unit composition to create a dramatic and fulfilling gameplay experience.
