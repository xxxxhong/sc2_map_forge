# StarCraft II Scenario: "The Proxy Gambit"

## Scenario Overview
**Theme:** 借刀杀人 (Kill with a Borrowed Knife) – The player must manipulate enemy forces into destroying each other rather than engaging directly.

**Map Context:** The battle takes place in a narrow mining canyon with a central neutral Zerg infestation separating Terran and Protoss forward positions.

---

## Force Composition

### Player (Terran)
- 1x **Ghost** (with Cloak and Snipe researched)
- 2x **Reapers** (with KD-8 Charge researched)
- **Hidden asset:** 1x **Neutral Broodling nest** (triggered by terrain interaction)

### Enemy (Protoss)
- 2x **Stalkers** (with Blink)
- 1x **High Templar** (with Psionic Storm)
- 2x **Zealots** (with Charge)
- **Hidden threat:** 1x **Warp Prism** (stationary, containing 2 additional Zealots in reserve)

---

## Positioning & Phases

### Initial Setup:
```
[Terran Start]━━━━[Narrow Bridge]━━━━[Zerg Infestation]━━━━[Protoss Outpost]━━(Cliff)
       ↑                    ↑                  ↑                    ↑
    Ghost +           Reaper #1          Broodling           Stalker x2
    Reaper #2                             Nest              High Templar
                                                           Zealot x2 (patrolling)
                                                           Warp Prism (hidden)
```

### Phase 1: Infiltration
- Player starts at southwest base entrance.
- Ghost must snipe a **Zerg Aberration** (neutral) guarding the broodling nest without alerting Protoss patrols.
- Reapers scout flanks, identifying:
  - Protoss patrol routes (30-second cycles)
  - Warp Prism location (cliff edge)
  - High Templar's stationary position near infestation

### Phase 2: Provocation
- Ghost uses **"Distraction Pulse"** (special ability for this scenario) on the Zerg nest.
- This triggers broodlings to swarm toward the *nearest energy source* – the Protoss Warp Prism.
- Protoss forces automatically engage the Zerg, pulling all units from their positions.

### Phase 3: Escalation
- As Protoss cleans up the Zerg, the High Templar uses Psionic Storm, damaging nearby Stalkers.
- Player's Ghost must **snipe the High Templar at 50% health** – making it appear the Zerg killed it.
- Remaining Protoss forces (now damaged) turn on each other due to **"Neural Parasite"** (special debuff from Zerg nest).

### Phase 4: Cleanup
- Player uses Reapers to:
  - KD-8 Charge the weakened Stalkers
  - Kite remaining Zealots into residual Zerg creep (slows movement)
- Ghost finishes final unit with snipe.

---

## Victory/Defeat Conditions

### Victory:
- All Protoss forces eliminated
- Warp Prism destroyed
- **At least 2 Protoss units must be killed by other Protoss or neutral forces**
- Ghost survives

### Defeat:
- Ghost dies
- Reapers both die before Phase 4
- Player directly kills more than 3 Protoss units before Phase 4
- Warp Prism escapes (if not destroyed in 8 minutes)

---

## Terrain Utilization Notes

### Key Map Features (from uploaded terrain):
1. **Central Zerg Infestation**
   - Neutral units attack nearest energy signature
   - Creep slows non-Zerg units by 30%
   - Use to split Protoss forces

2. **Narrow Bridge**
   - Choke point for Reaper hit-and-run
   - Line-of-sight break for Ghost cloak

3. **Cliff Edge near Warp Prism**
   - Elevated position allows overlooking infestation
   - Reapers can use cliff jumps to bypass melee Zealots

4. **Destroyable Rock Formations**
   - Two rocks seal alternate paths
   - Can be blown to redirect Zerg swarm or trap Protoss

### Advanced Tactics:
- **Snipe Timing:** Ghost must snipe the High Templar *during* Psionic Storm animation to hide kill credit.
- **Reaper Mining Drill:** Use KD-8 Charge on mineral patches near Protoss to create debris that blocks Zealot charges.
- **Warp Prisor:** If player destroys Warp Prism too early, reinforcements won't arrive to fight Zerg – must wait until Zealots deploy.

---

## Designer Notes
- **借刀杀人 Execution:** Player's damage contribution must be <40% of total damage to Protoss forces.
- **Audio Cues:** Provide distinct sound when Protoss units damage each other (feedback for stratagem working).
- **Optional Objective:** If player completes scenario without losing units and with 0 direct kills, unlock "Master Tactician" achievement.

**Balance Tip:** Adjust Zerg swarm size based on difficulty – more Zerg means easier proxy battle but more cleanup required afterward.
