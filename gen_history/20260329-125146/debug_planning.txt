# StarCraft II Scenario: "The Proxy's Gambit"

## Scenario Overview
**Stratagem:** 借刀杀人 (Kill with a Borrowed Knife)  
**Theme:** Manipulate the Protoss forces into destroying each other while preserving your limited Terran forces.

## Force Composition

### Player (Terran) - 10 Units:
- **4x Ghost** (Cloaked, with EMP and Snipe)
- **2x Raven** (Auto-Turrets, Interference Matrix)
- **3x Hellion** (Scout/Harass capability)
- **1x SCV** (For emergency repairs/decoy)

### Enemy (Protoss) - 10 Units (Two Hostile Factions):
**Golden Armada Loyalists (5 units):**
- 2x Zealot
- 1x Stalker
- 1x Sentry
- 1x High Templar

**Tal'darim Renegades (5 units):**
- 2x Adept
- 1x Immortal
- 1x Disruptor
- 1x Dark Templar (Cloaked)

## Terrain Utilization Notes
Based on the uploaded map file analysis:
1. **Central Choke Point:** Narrow pass with high ground on both sides - ideal for ambushes
2. **Two Enemy Bases:** Separated by a destructible rock barrier (key terrain feature)
3. **Vision Towers:** One overlooking each Protoss encampment
4. **Mineral Line:** Between the two Protoss factions - a contested resource
5. **Cliff Edges:** Multiple elevation changes for tactical positioning

## Positioning & Phases

### Initial Setup:
- **Player Forces:** Start on high ground overlooking the central choke point
- **Golden Armada:** Positioned west of the choke, guarding their mineral line
- **Tal'darim:** Positioned east of the choke, separated by destructible rocks
- **Destructible Rocks:** Currently block direct engagement between Protoss factions

### Phase 1: Intelligence Gathering (0-2 minutes)
- Use Hellions to scout both Protoss positions
- Ravens deploy Auto-Turrets near vision towers to gain map control
- Ghosts remain cloaked, observing enemy patrol patterns

### Phase 2: Provocation (2-5 minutes)
**Key Stratagem Execution:**
1. Ghosts use Snipe to damage Tal'darim units from cloak
2. Immediately retreat toward Golden Armada position
3. Use EMP on Golden Armada Sentry to drain energy (preventing Guardian Shield)
4. Raven uses Interference Matrix on Golden Armada Stalker
5. SCV acts as decoy, drawing Tal'darim pursuit toward Golden Armada

### Phase 3: The Borrowed Knife (5-8 minutes)
**Critical Actions:**
1. Ghosts destroy the destructible rocks separating the two Protoss factions
2. Raven drops Auto-Turrets between the now-engaged Protoss forces
3. Ghosts EMP both sides' shields equally
4. Hellions flank to clean up weakened survivors

### Phase 4: Mop-up (8-10 minutes)
- Use remaining Ghosts to Snipe any high-value survivors
- Ravens disable remaining dangerous units (Disruptor/High Templar)
- Hellions chase down fleeing units

## Victory/Defeat Conditions

### Victory:
- Both Protoss factions eliminate each other
- Player retains at least 6 original units
- No Protoss units remain by 10-minute mark

### Defeat:
- Player loses more than 5 units
- Either Protoss faction retains more than 3 units after engagement
- Player fails to provoke conflict within 6 minutes

## Special Mechanics & Triggers

1. **Aggro Manipulation:** Damaging a unit while cloaked near their enemy will cause them to blame the nearest visible faction
2. **Destructible Rocks:** Can only be destroyed after both factions have been provoked
3. **Vision Control:** Controlling both vision towers reveals entire engagement area
4. **Energy Management:** Ghosts have limited energy - must choose between EMP and Cloak strategically

## Player Tips
1. **Timing is Critical:** Provoke both factions simultaneously to ensure mutual aggression
2. **Positioning:** Keep Ravens behind Ghosts for detection and support
3. **Resource Denial:** Use Hellions to harass mineral lines, increasing enemy desperation
4. **The Decoy:** The SCV is expendable but most effective when sacrificed at the perfect moment to trigger full-scale conflict

## Narrative Context
*"Command, this is Spectre Team. We've identified two hostile Protoss factions in the area. The Tal'darim renegades and Golden Armada loyalists are unaware of each other's presence. Our orders: make sure they become intimately acquainted. Let their zealotry do our work for us."*

This scenario emphasizes the 借刀杀人 stratagem by forcing players to manipulate enemies rather than engage directly, using terrain features and unit abilities to orchestrate conflict between superior forces.
